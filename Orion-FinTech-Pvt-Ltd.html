Project Overview

Project Overview: This project focused on securing the corporate headquarters of Orion FinTech Pvt. Ltd., located in Bengaluru, Karnataka. Orion is an emerging leader in the Financial Technology sector, and the project was aimed at strengthening their security infrastructure to safeguard sensitive client data and maintain regulatory compliance. The scope included the installation of high-resolution IP surveillance cameras, smart biometric access control systems, and AI-powered monitoring protocols.

Client & Service Details:
Orion FinTech Pvt. Ltd. engaged us to implement a 360° physical security solution across multiple floors of their office. Our services included:

Deployment Highlights:

  • Installation of 110+ AI-enabled IP cameras for 24/7 surveillance
  • Integration of RFID & biometric-based access control on all entry/exit points
  • Setup of a centralized monitoring system with real-time anomaly detection
  • Compliance with data protection and financial security regulations

Market Analysis: The FinTech industry demands elevated standards of operational security. Our partnership with Orion showcases:
Innovative Technology: Real-time alerting systems and facial recognition improve facility responsiveness.
Regulatory Assurance: Our deployments support RBI-compliant security frameworks for data-rich environments.
Comprehensive Coverage: Every floor and access zone is secured, ensuring uniform standards across departments.
Operational Excellence: Security efficiency improved employee movement flow and protected critical systems.

Challenges

  1. Integrating advanced surveillance systems into the existing infrastructure without disrupting operations.
  2. Ensuring seamless access control mechanisms for a large workforce with diverse security clearance levels.
  3. Maintaining system reliability during peak operational hours to avoid any vulnerabilities or downtime.
  4. Implementing robust data protection measures to safeguard sensitive facility information.
  5. Training employees on new security protocols while minimizing disruptions to daily operations.

Solutions

  1. Deployed modular and scalable surveillance systems, allowing integration without interrupting ongoing operations.
  2. Implemented advanced biometric and RFID-based access control systems to manage diverse clearance levels effectively.
  3. Installed high-reliability equipment with failover mechanisms to ensure continuous performance during peak hours.
  4. Adopted encrypted data storage and transfer protocols to protect sensitive facility and employee information. For additional details, click here.
  5. Conducted interactive training sessions and provided step-by-step guides to ensure smooth adoption of new protocols.
